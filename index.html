Hello. My name is Mark Finger, and I make web stuff.

I’m currently floating about Sydney and working on some tech projects that interest me. Part of my time is spent coding at the Interaction Consortium.

You can find me on github or send me some email.

Circa 2014, I had the pleasure of working with The Global Mail, a now defunct media organisation. While a lot of our work has been lost to time, I've been able to rebuild some of the stories that we were involved with.

In particular, the following pieces were experimental takes on journalism through mixed media.

  • The Crowded Desert Our crowded desert: 40 km per person and 20 people to a house
  • At Work Inside Our Detention Centres: A Guard’s Story An illustrated interview from inside Australia’s immigration detention system
  • The Town That Wouldn’t Disappear One Australian mining community is putting coal on trial
  • The Great Pill Pu$h Tracking the marketing of medicines in Australia
  • How Not To Win A War Sri Lanka’s paradise betrayed by monks, thugs and smugglers
  • Food Fights Flashpoints in the GMO Debate
